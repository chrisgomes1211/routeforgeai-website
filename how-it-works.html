The Pipeline

How RouteForge AI works

Five specialist agents. One orchestrator. A seamless pipeline from audit to action.

🔍 Researcher

Yellow — Researcher & Analyst

Intelligence

Scans market data, analyses your current operations, and evaluates compliance posture. Delivers structured research briefs, competitor analyses, and data summaries. In evaluation mode, it produces KILL / PIVOT / SCALE recommendations with supporting evidence.

🎨 Designer

Red-Orange — Designer

Solutions & Architecture

Turns research insights into actionable system designs. Creates wireframes, user flows, architecture documents, and detailed design specs. Ensures every solution is practical for a small business budget and timeline.

💻 Maker

Blue — Maker

Building & Deployment

Builds the actual systems: dashboards, analytics tools, AI models, and integrations. Writes production-ready code, runs tests, and handles deployment. Works strictly from the Designer's specifications.

🚀 Marketer

Green — Marketer

Distribution & Growth

Creates launch campaigns, email sequences, social media assets, and growth strategies. Ensures your AI-powered insights translate into real customer engagement and revenue.

👑 Manager

Purple — Manager (The Octopus)

Orchestration

The central nervous system. Decomposes your goals into tasks, dispatches the right agents, enforces quality gates between stages, and synthesises outputs into a coherent strategy you can act on.

Orchestration Modes

RouteForge AI adapts to your needs with three powerful orchestration patterns.

🕵

Sequential Pipeline

Each agent passes its output to the next. Perfect for end-to-end projects where each phase depends on the last. Research → Design → Build → Market.

👥

Parallel Fan-Out

Multiple agents work simultaneously. Research runs competitor analysis while Designer prototypes a solution. Speed without sacrificing quality.

🔄

Review Loop

Iterative refinement between agents. Maker builds, Designer reviews, Maker revises. Repeat until every quality gate passes.
