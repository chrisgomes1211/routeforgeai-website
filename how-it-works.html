Meet Your
AI Team

Five specialised AI agents working together to turn your data into growth.

01

Researcher

Analyses market trends, customer behaviour, and historical data

Researcher AI

Scans market data, analyses past sales, identifies patterns and seasonality. Detects emerging trends and local events that impact demand.

02

Designer

Optimises menu pricing, layout, and product mix based on data

Designer AI

Analyses menu performance, suggests pricing adjustments, and recommends product mix changes to maximise margin without sacrificing volume.

03

Maker

Generates demand forecasts, staff schedules, and inventory plans

Maker AI

Takes insights from Researcher and Designer to generate daily demand forecasts, optimal staff rotas, and smart purchasing plans that minimise waste.

04

Marketer

Tracks campaign performance and customer engagement

Marketer AI

Monitors marketing ROI, tracks customer acquisition costs, identifies your most effective channels, and recommends where to invest your marketing budget.

05

Manager

Orchestrates all agents and provides unified recommendations

Manager AI

The orchestrator. Combines outputs from all agents, resolves conflicts, and presents a unified dashboard with clear, prioritised recommendations for your business.
