Real Problems, Real Solutions

F&B challenges RouteForge solves

Six critical pain points Dublin food businesses face — and how our AI agents address each one.

🍲
Challenge

Food Waste

Irish food businesses lose thousands of euros annually to spoilage and over-ordering. Without demand forecasting, waste is accepted as a cost of doing business.

AI predicts demand within 8% accuracy, slashing waste by up to 30%
💼
Challenge

Staffing & Scheduling

Labour is the largest cost after food. Understaffing loses customers; overstaffing burns margin. Most cafes and restaurants guess their schedules.

AI analyses foot traffic patterns to optimise shift schedules in minutes
🧡
Challenge

Customer Loyalty

Punch cards and generic email blasts don't work anymore. Dublin diners expect personalised experiences but SMEs lack the data infrastructure to deliver them.

AI segments customers and automates personalised retention campaigns
💰
Challenge

Dynamic Pricing

Competitors change prices daily. Without real-time market intelligence, businesses leave money on the table or price themselves out of the market.

AI monitors competitors and recommends optimal pricing by menu item
📱
Challenge

Online Orders

Delivery platforms take 30% commission. SMEs need their own ordering channels but lack the technical expertise and budget to build them.

AI-powered direct ordering system with intelligent upselling built in
⭐
Challenge

Reviews & Reputation

One bad review can cost weeks of revenue. Most SMEs don't have the bandwidth to monitor, respond, or analyse review patterns across platforms.

AI monitors all review platforms and generates brand-aligned responses
