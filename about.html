Our Journey

From Concept to
Intelligence Platform

★
January 2026
The Idea
Recognised that F&B SMEs were being left behind by AI. ▼

We noticed that while enterprise chains had data science teams, the local cafe or family restaurant had zero access to AI-powered insights. That gap became our mission.

🔍
February 2026
Research & Discovery
Conducted 30+ interviews with F&B operators across Ireland. ▼

The #1 pain point? Demand unpredictability. 78% of operators said they had no forecasting tool and relied purely on intuition for staffing and purchasing decisions.

⚙
March 2026
First Prototype
Built the first working prototype combining AI with a simple dashboard. ▼

3 weeks of sprint development. The early model achieved 89% forecast accuracy using just 2 weeks of POS data. We knew we were onto something.

🚀
April 2026
Private Beta
Launched with 5 partner cafes in Dublin. ▼

Results exceeded expectations: 22% average waste reduction, 12% staff cost savings, and 94% forecast accuracy by week 4. All 5 partners opted to continue.

✓
Today
Public Launch
RouteForge AI is now available to every F&B business. ▼

Still early, still iterating. We're actively onboarding new customers and shipping weekly improvements. Your feedback shapes what we build next.

AI-powered intelligence for the food & beverage industry.
