Our Journey

From Concept to
Intelligence Platform

January 2026
The Idea
Recognised that F&B SMEs were being left behind by AI. Most tools were built for enterprise, not for the local cafe or restaurant.
February 2026
Research & Discovery
Conducted 30+ interviews with cafe owners, restaurant managers, and F&B operators across Ireland to understand their biggest operational pain points.
March 2026
First Prototype
Built the first working prototype combining AI demand forecasting with a simple, non-technical dashboard interface.
April 2026
Private Beta
Launched private beta with 5 partner cafes in Dublin. Initial results showed 22% waste reduction and 12% staff cost savings.
Today
Public Launch
RouteForge AI is now available to F&B businesses everywhere. Still early, still iterating — and we'd love your feedback.
