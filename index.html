FAQ

Questions? We've
Got Answers

What data does RouteForge need to get started?+

Just your POS sales data and staff schedules. We'll guide you through connecting everything in under 15 minutes. Inventory data is optional but recommended for waste tracking.

How accurate are the demand forecasts?+

Our models typically achieve 94%+ accuracy after the initial 2-week learning period. Accuracy improves over time as the AI learns your specific patterns, seasonality, and local events.

Is my data secure and GDPR compliant?+

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are fully GDPR compliant, EU-hosted, and never share your data with third parties. We also comply with the EU AI Act requirements.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your data is yours and can be exported at any time.

How is this different from my current POS analytics?+

Most POS systems show you what happened. RouteForge predicts what will happen and tells you exactly what to do about it — from optimal staff levels to purchasing quantities to pricing recommendations.
