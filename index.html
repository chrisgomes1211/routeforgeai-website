The Problem

Why Dublin's F&B SMEs are stuck

Despite living in a tech capital, most food and beverage businesses struggle to adopt AI meaningfully.

⚠

EU AI Act Uncertainty

New regulations create confusion. Most SMEs don't know if their AI usage is compliant or what they need to change.

📈

No Data Strategy

Customer data is siloed across spreadsheets, loyalty cards, and delivery apps. No unified view means no actionable insights.

💰

Cost of Entry

Enterprise AI solutions are priced for multinationals. Independent restaurants and cafes can't justify the investment.

💪

Staff & Skills Gap

Hiring data scientists or AI specialists is out of reach. Existing teams are already stretched running daily operations.

The Solution

How RouteForge AI works

A three-phase system that takes you from audit to AI-powered growth.

1

Assess & Audit

Our Researcher Agent analyses your current operations, data maturity, and compliance posture. You get a clear picture of where you stand.

2

Strategise & Build

The Designer and Maker agents collaborate to create a tailored AI roadmap. From customer analytics to waste reduction systems — built for your business.

3

Deploy & Grow

The Marketer Agent helps you launch AI-powered campaigns and customer experiences. Continuous evaluation ensures you stay compliant and competitive.
